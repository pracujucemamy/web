O firme

Medzi naše hlavné úlohy patrí návrh, vývoj a správa softvéru, implementácia a údržba SAP riešení, nezávislé testovanie a zabezpečovanie kvality IT služieb v rámci energetického sektora. Zaoberáme sa aj inovatívnymi riešeniami v obasti e-mobility a smart home.

Náplň práce

We are looking for SAP PM Consultant, who will join our team. Team consists of SAP consultants and ABAP developers who support daily operations of the customers and provide new solutions to make their business better. As part of this team, your main responsibility will be close cooperation with developers in ongoing projects and providing support in the configuration and business processes in implementation of SAP solutions.

The role of SAP PM Consultant includes:

  • providing support in the configuration and business processes in implementation of SAP solutions
  • customizing of various systems based on customer requirements
  • 2nd a 3rd level support for users
  • monitoring of SAP systems
  • functional testing of implemented SAP solutions
  • communication with customers and ABAP developers on daily basis
  • participation in projects

Požiadavky

  • SAP PM user experience will be an advantage
  • you are familiar with plant maintenance processes
  • you have analytical thinking
  • you speak German and English fluently
