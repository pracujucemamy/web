O firme

Tesco nie je len miesto kam chodíte nakupovať. Je to omnoho viac. Za všetkým stojí tvrdá práca našich kolegov. V Tescu pracujeme ako jeden tím, ktorý sa snaží zákazníkom prinášať každý deň niečo navyše. V rámci Tesco skupiny budujeme inkluzívne miesto na prácu, kde sú všetci kolegovia oceňovaní za to akí sú, bez ohľadu na kultúru, z ktorej pochádzajú a ktorí nám pomáhajú budovať úspešné podnikanie a odrážajú rozmanitosť komunít, ktorým slúžime.

Náplň práce

Aká bude vaša práca u nás?

  • Builds a broad and deep Business performance understanding and its drivers.
  • Analyses data from multiple sources (both internal and external) to create actionable insight.
  • Monitors market dynamics and competition activities and understands their influence on Tesco performance.
  • Supports Customer Plan and regular business processes with market data including presenting the results (event evaluation, range reviews, category resets, category planning, trading meetings, customer review, own brand new product development).
  • Delivering recommendations and insights to Product and Customer teams by creating one-off or regular materials and giving structured presentations.
  • Builds market data sources expertise and consultancy (i.e. Euromonitor, Mintel, GlobalData, Statistical offices).
  • Tracking and researching available content as well as preparing information on request.
  • Nielsen and GfK market data (tools) consultancy both for Customer and Product departments.
  • Consultancy focused on describing usage and functionalities of available tools as well as helping understand the results from commercial point of view.
  • Provides trainings for stakeholders to increase knowledge and usage of our data sources
