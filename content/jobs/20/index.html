O firme

Tesco nie je len miesto kam chodíte nakupovať. Je to omnoho viac. Za všetkým stojí tvrdá práca našich kolegov. V Tescu pracujeme ako jeden tím, ktorý sa snaží zákazníkom prinášať každý deň niečo navyše. V rámci Tesco skupiny budujeme inkluzívne miesto na prácu, kde sú všetci kolegovia oceňovaní za to akí sú, bez ohľadu na kultúru, z ktorej pochádzajú a ktorí nám pomáhajú budovať úspešné podnikanie a odrážajú rozmanitosť komunít, ktorým slúžime.

Náplň práce

  • Work on developing storytelling to translate research into answers for our stakeholders’ business questions.
  • Collaborate with stakeholders on research briefs, and then lead research projects from beginning to end - ensure these deliver against requirements, and are presented in a way to make the complex simple and land the key messages with impact.
  • Analyse data sets of multiple types (research, behavioural, economic) to generate insight about our customers, competitors and markets that we can act upon.
  • Use expertise to produce actionable insight to support our customer growth strategy within a fast-paced environment.
  • Cooperates closely with research agencies to ensure high level content is delivered that is in line with research brief and business requirements.
  • Collaborate with DH Team on preparing, conducting and turning into insights outcomes of researches on Shopper Thoughts Panels.
  • Takes responsibility for delivering Customer Experience insight on a country level
  • Monitors the brand health and image of Tesco vs. local competitors.
  • Pro-actively look for new research techniques to understand customer needs and motivations in partnership with other Tesco teams and our research agencies.
  • Manages local research budget.
