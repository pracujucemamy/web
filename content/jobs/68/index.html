O firme

Established in 1951, IOM is a Related Organization of the United Nations, and as the leading UN agency in the field of migration, works closely with governmental, intergovernmental and non-governmental partners. IOM is dedicated to promoting humane and orderly migration for the benefit of all. It does so by providing services and advice to governments and migrants. www.iom.sk

Náplň práce

Under the overall supervision of the National Resources Management Officer in IOM Slovakia office and direct supervision of the Head of Finance Sub-Unit; and, in collaboration with relevant units locally and at Headquarters, the successful candidate will be responsible and accountable for financial, budgetary and administrative support to the efficient office functioning.

Core Functions / Responsibilities:

  • Assist in managing financial resources in accordance with IOM rules and regulations;
  • Monitor compliance with IOM financial policies, procedures, rules and regulations;
  • Perform general ledger accounting and ensure all transactions are supported by legitimate and sufficient documentation and proper authorization;
  • Assist with monthly and yearly accounting closures;
  • Assist with preparation of budgets and other reports as required;
  • Provide regular and ad hoc financial information to support informed financial decision making;
  • Provide guidance in accounting, budget and financial related issues;
  • Monitor expenditures to ensure they remain within authorized levels;
  • Assist with overhead cost allocation;
  • Ensure disbursements are made based on proper authorizations and are supported by legitimate and sufficient documentation;
  • Monitor bank reconciliations for IOM bank accounts are regularly performed and reviewed by designated stakeholders;
  • Provide technical support and guidance to all staff regarding travel, expense claims, document retrieval, etc.;
  • Provide assistance in responding to audit queries and follow up on audit recommendations;
  • Perform other related duties as required.
