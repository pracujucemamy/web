Náplň práce

PwC is well known in connection with big4, audit, tax and consulting. However, we have experts in the field of technology consulting, who deal with many areas through business process management, robotics and automation to artificial intelligence.

We have clients and projects all over the world. Our clients are mainly large financial institutions - banks and insurance companies, but we also have a project in the field of healthcare.

Our biggest focus is BPM. Technologies we are using for our international clients are Pega and Appian.

Never heard of it? It is absolutely ok :) We are focusing on the international market where these technologies are strongly demanded.

In the beginning you can expect intensive training, where you will learn everything necessary to become an Appian or Pega developer.

What training involves:

  • database recap,
  • webinars and video-trainings oriented on consulting skills,
  • Appian / Pega essentials trainings,
  • access to training cloud where you can try everything you learn,
  • regular sessions with seniors developers from the team,
  • international certification (paid by PwC),
  • shadowing on the project in the beginning.

When everything goes well after the first months you will be fully dedicated to one of our current projects as an independent developer.

Key responsibilities of Appian / Pega developer:

  • coordinating and delivering work at a high level of quality and on time,
  • design, development, testing and implementation of BPM solutions based on Appian / Pega software,
  • be in touch with front-end, back-end, databases, integrations, plug-ins etc.,
  • working in agile methodology (mostly Scrum),
  • communicating with other team members, senior colleagues and clients.
